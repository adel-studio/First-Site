About Adel

Adel is a furniture concierge that helps shoppers make faster, personalized design decisions. We connect users with high-quality furniture retailers through affiliate feeds and approved partner integrations. Our goal is to simplify the shopping experience by curating upscale brands and providing tailored recommendations that save time and reduce overwhelm.

Our audience is design-conscious, digitally native households who want high-quality pieces without the fatigue of endless browsing.
